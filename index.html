EUCOR Master in Computer Science

Program Objectives (Work in Progress)

The EUCOR Master in Computer Science is a trinational and bilingual (English/French/German) program currently under development. Its main goal is to offer advanced academic training at the intersection of computer science and interdisciplinary domains. The program emphasizes research, innovation, and the ethical application of artificial intelligence across different sectors of society.
